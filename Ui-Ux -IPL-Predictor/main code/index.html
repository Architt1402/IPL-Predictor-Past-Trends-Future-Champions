Most IPL Titles

Mumbai Indians (MI) have won the IPL 5 times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020), followed by Chennai Super Kings (CSK) with 5 titles (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).

Top Run-Scorer

Virat Kohli holds the record for most runs in IPL history with over 6,283 runs as of 2022, playing for Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB).

Most Wickets

Yuzvendra Chahal leads the IPL wicket charts with 205 wickets, followed by Piyush Chawla with 192 wickets. Bravo holds third place with 183 wickets, while Malinga has 170 wickets..

Highest Individual Score

Chris Gayle scored 175* off 66 balls for RCB against Pune Warriors in 2013, the highest individual score in IPL history.

First IPL Match

The first-ever IPL match was played on April 18, 2008, between Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) and Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB). KKR won by 140 runs!

Youngest Player

As of May 2025, the record for the youngest player to debut in the Indian Premier League (IPL) is held by Vaibhav Suryavanshi, who made his debut for the Rajasthan Royals on April 19, 2025, at the age of 14 years and 23 days.

Biggest Win

MI holds the record for the biggest win by runs, defeating Delhi Daredevils (now Delhi Capitals) by 146 runs in 2017.
